Ivan Sipiran

Assistant Professor · Department of Computer Science
University of Chile, Santiago

I am a computer scientist working at the intersection of 3D shape analysis, geometry processing, and computer vision. My research also explores computational approaches to cultural heritage and archaeology. Previously, I was a researcher at PUCP (Lima, Perú) and held a post-doctoral position at the University of Konstanz.
